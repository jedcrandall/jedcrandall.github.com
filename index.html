A few highlights to give you some idea of what my research group does...

We cross the wires in VPNs, to demonstrate the lack of safety properties that at-risk users need.

We track Internet censorship and surveillance, to bring transparency to it and help fight against it.

We offer our technical expertise to journalists and activists whenever we can, e.g., by answering questions in press conferences. The Bhima Koregaon 16 were framed!

We work with our collaborators to measure information controls on the Internet and track concerning developments, such as Russia's TSPU.

We attack the technologies that at-risk users depend upon the most, so that vulnerabilities that put them in danger become known before it's too late.

We search for inspiration and ideas from the hackers of the previous millennium. SEND IN THE CLONER!

We do award-winning science to understand surveillance and censorship from the perspective of an adversarial Internet infrastructure.

By following social media and reverse engineering apps, we collect and track lists of dirty words, in Chinese and other languages.

We empower the next generation of hackers, journalists, and activists through education and outreach. Hack the planet!
